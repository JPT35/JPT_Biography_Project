Biography

objective of this webpage is to present a biography for Lamar Demeatrice Jackson

Early Life

Lamar Jackson, was born in Pompano Beach, Florida to Felicia Jones and Lamar Jackson Sr.

Jackson was a standout at Boyton Beach High School, located in Boyton Beach Florida

Jackson was a 3 star recruit coming out of high school, and only visited 4 schools before deciding to attend the University of Louisville

College Accolades
  • Unanimous All-American (2016)
  • Heisman Trophy Winner (2016)
  • 2 time ACC player of the year (2017,2017)
  • ACC Athlete of the year (2018)
  • Louisville Cardinals Ring of Honor
  • Louisville Cardinals number 8 retired
NFL Accolades
  • Drafted number 28 overall in 2018 NFL Draft
  • First Team All-Pro (2019)
  • 2x Pro-Bowl (2019,2021)
  • NFL MVP (2019)
NFL Records
